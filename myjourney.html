My Journey

Year 1

Starting my journey at Thammasat University as a CSTU student, I have learned many new things like programming langauge such as HTML, CSS, C++ and more. I have a many new friends when im entered university.

Year 2

In the second year, I expect to dive deeper into advanced concepts like data structures, object-oriented programming, and databases. By then, I aim to be comfortable writing more efficient code and tackling complex problems. I will work on larger personal projects, such as developing simple web applications or engaging in collaborative projects with my peers.

Year 3

The third year will mark a turning point as I begin to specialize in specific fields that align with my interests and career goals. Whether it’s artificial intelligence, data science, or software development, I plan to focus on building in-depth knowledge and skills. This year, I also aim to secure an internship or work on industry-related projects to gain practical experience in a real-world environment.

Year 4

In my final year, I plan to bring everything together by working on a significant capstone project that will showcase my learning and abilities. This project will likely be focused on solving a real-world problem using the skills I've accumulated throughout my studies, whether that involves developing an AI-driven solution, building a scalable web application, or addressing a cybersecurity challenge.
